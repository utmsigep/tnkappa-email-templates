Support the Balanced Man Scholarship

Why donate to the Balanced Man Scholarship?

Your tax-deductible gift (through the Sigma Phi Epsilon Educational Foundation) to our Direct Scholarship Fund allows the chapter to give a number of $500 scholarships for incoming male students. It is an opportunity to recognize their accomplishments in high school and introduce them to campus life at UT Martin.

The undergraduate members interview and select the winners. While recipients are not in any way obligated to join Sigma Phi Epsilon, we have found over the years that our fraternity's ideals align closely with those selected, and many will seek out membership. As such, it is our most potent recruiting tool available.

We appreciate your support!
